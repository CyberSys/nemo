Information Sources from the NSSDC's Project STELAR

There are zillions of information sources out there in the Internet. Here are some that might be interesting...
Directory of NASA WAIS servers
The directory of WAIS servers running at NASA/Goddard Space Flight Center
STELAR Products
WAIS sources available from the STELAR Project
FTP to hypatia
Anonymous FTP connection to hypatia - home machine of STELAR and the Astronomical Data center
NODIS
The NSSDC's NODIS account, providing access to the NSSDC's on-line services, including the NASA and Global Change Master Directory, NSSDC and ADC data archives and CD-ROMs.
STEIS
The Space Telescope Science Institute Electronic Information System gopher server.
UU-NNA Astronomy listings
The Usenet University - New Network Academy Meta-Library listing of on-line astronomy resources
UU-NNA Homepage
The Usenet University - New Network Academy Meta-Library
C.U.Q. FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions from comp.unix.questions
Internet FYIs
The Internet FYI (For Your Information) index.
Archie
The Archie lookup service, useful for finding files available for anonymous ftp (if you know part of the file name).
WAIS
The WAIS Directory of Servers. Allows you to do full text searches on many databases.
Gopher holes
A list of all known gopher servers (more about gopher ).
Hytelnet
Hypertext database of telnet information services (library catalogues and others). The library catalogues are arranged geographically, and the others are arranged by function.
The World Wide Web
Information about WWW (also known as W3), a globally distributed hypertext system.
